New paragraph

An orphan is cured more easily, by inserting a blank line or
forcing a page break to push the orphan line onto
the next page to be with the rest of
its paragraph. Such a cure may have to be undone
if editing the text repositions the automatic page or column break.
Similarly, a single orphaned
word at the end of a paragraph can be cured by forcing one or
more words from the preceding line into the orphan's line.
